Why This Project

"

蓝色小嗵的形象来源于唱作组合 DOUDOU（杜冰儿、杜飞儿）的歌曲《嗵嗵》。

XIAOTONG is inspired by the song "Tong Tong" from singer-songwriter duo DOUDOU (Du Bing'er & Du Fei'er).

「嗵」是森林里果实落地的声响，也是生命轮转、生生不息的回响。这首歌讲的不是告别，而是每一次落下都意味着重新开始。

"Tong" is the sound of fruit falling in the forest — an echo of life's endless cycle. The song isn't about farewell; every fall marks a new beginning.

我很喜欢这个意象，所以把 TA 变成了一只住在桌面上的小精灵——TA 从森林里来，带着那声「嗵」，留在你身边。

I loved this imagery, so I turned it into a little spirit living on your desktop — TA came from the forest, carrying that sound of "tong", and stayed by your side.
